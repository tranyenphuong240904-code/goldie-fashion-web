About Goldie

Our Story

Goldie Vietnam is a contemporary fashion brand created for young women who appreciate femininity, elegance and modern lifestyle.

Inspired by everyday moments, Goldie focuses on wearable designs that balance aesthetics and comfort, allowing women to express themselves naturally through fashion.

Brand Vision

Goldie aims to become a lifestyle fashion brand that connects emotions, creativity and modern women through visual storytelling and seasonal collections.
